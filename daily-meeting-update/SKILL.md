      # Daily Meeting Update

      Generate concise daily updates for standups and leadership meetings.

      ## Usage

      - Compile daily meeting updates from calendar, email, and Obsidian notes
      - Format updates for standup meetings (what I did, what I'm doing, blockers)
      - Summarize key decisions and action items from the day
      - Generate status reports for leadership reviews
