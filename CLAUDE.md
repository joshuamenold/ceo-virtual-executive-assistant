# CEO Virtual Executive Assistant

Turn Claude Code + Obsidian into your virtual executive assistant. Automate email, calendar, Slack, notes, task management, web research, and daily workflows — so you can focus on leading, not admin.

## How This Stack Works

This stack gives you a virtual executive assistant powered by Claude Code. It connects to your key tools — Gmail, Google Calendar, Google Docs, Google Drive, Slack, Obsidian, and web search — so you can manage your day with natural language commands.

## Core Capabilities

### Communication
- **Email**: Read, triage, compose, and send emails via Gmail. Create filters to auto-organize.
- **Slack**: Send messages, check channels, and stay in the loop without context-switching.

### Scheduling
- **Calendar**: View your agenda, find free time, and create events in Google Calendar.
- **Meeting Prep**: Get daily briefings and meeting updates automatically.

### Knowledge & Notes
- **Obsidian**: Create, edit, search, and organize notes in your Obsidian vault using markdown.
- **Bases & Databases**: Use Obsidian's database-like features for structured tracking.

### Documents & Files
- **Google Docs**: Read and write documents directly.
- **Google Drive**: Browse and manage files in Drive.
- **Presentations**: Generate slide decks from outlines or data.

### Research & Web
- **Tavily Search**: Run web searches to pull in current information for decisions or briefings.

### Productivity Workflows
- **Writing Plans**: Structure long-form writing with outlines and plans.
- **Daily Meeting Updates**: Generate concise updates for standup or leadership meetings.

## Quick Start

Ask me things like:
- "What's on my calendar today?"
- "Triage my inbox and flag anything urgent"
- "Draft a reply to the email from Sarah about Q2 planning"
- "Create a note in Obsidian summarizing today's meetings"
- "Find 30 minutes free this week for a 1:1 with marketing"
- "Search the web for the latest on our competitor's product launch"
- "Create a presentation outline for the board meeting"
