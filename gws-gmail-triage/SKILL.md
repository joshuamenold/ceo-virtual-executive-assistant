    # Gmail Triage

    Triage and organize your inbox efficiently.

    ## Usage

    - Scan inbox and categorize emails by urgency and type
    - Flag important messages that need immediate attention
    - Summarize unread emails into a quick briefing
    - Suggest actions (reply, archive, delegate, follow-up)
